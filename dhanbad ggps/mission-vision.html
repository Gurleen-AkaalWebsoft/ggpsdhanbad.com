Vision

Our vision is to provide a stimulating learning environment with a technological orientation across the curriculum, which maximizes individual potential and ensures student’s ability levels at various spectrums. We are well equipped to meet the challenges of education, and to make every child say “Yes, I Can” through performance excellence, responsible leadership and respect for others.

The overarching objective of the Guru Gobind Singh Public School Dhanbad is to impart a joy of learning to the children who attend the school. This sense of enjoyment will bring many further benefits in its wake and will result in happy, fulfilled, high-performing children who are motivated and eager to learn. This goal will be achieved via a range of individual stimuli to bring about the greatest possible degree of development in the abilities of the children. This strategic approach aimed at fostering the progress of every child encompasses both individual attention and teaching. In addition to the areas of potential stated above we also address the development of personal and social skills such as confidence, problem solving, group work and mutual respect. Our teaching philosophy takes the necessary individual child quality standards fully into account by clearly distancing itself from the bureaucratic approach adopted in German schools. The regular teacher training we conduct lays the foundations for high-quality delivery of our curriculum in the classroom. The particular emphasis we place on training for teaching staff has its basis in the latest educational findings. The undergo training our teachers includes methodology and didactics as well as practice in dealing with children exhibiting challenging behaviour. Teaching methods are adapted on an ongoing basis to meet the potential of the children. This type of quality assurance within the field of teacher training represents a high-quality form of teaching delivery which has been successfully deployed in adult education for many years and is now set to establish new benchmarks in the school system. This is an approach which provides your child with a unique opportunity in terms of the way in which knowledge is imparted. The needs of every child are addressed and an environment is created in which every child feels comfortable.

mission

Our mission is to serve society through excellence in education. We always aim to define and continually refine, the absolute standard of excellence in the area of academics through:
• The best quality of education we provide
• The efficiency of our teaching methodologies and system
• Sincerity towards students ,parents society and nation

The Guru Gobind Singh Public School Dhanbad strives to be a school offering a broad and challenging educational programme to students from diverse cultural backgrounds. The Guru Gobind Singh Public School Dhanbad is committed to inspiring students to achieve the highest standards of intellectual and personal development through a stimulating and comprehensive curriculum. Within a caring, respectful, multicultural environment, the school is committed to instilling in each student a desire to learn, to take appropriate risks, and to accept challenges. The school community is committed to developing students who are resilient and adaptable, equipped with the knowledge, skills, and disposition to continue their education and become personally fulfilled, interdependent, socially responsible adults.

Our mission is to develop children with active and creative minds, a sense of understanding and compassion for others, and the courage to act on their beliefs. Therefore we emphasise the total development of each child: spiritual, moral, intellectual, social, emotional, and physical. To develop tomorrow’s globally responsible leaders as we strongly believe that – “Today’s Children are Tomorrow’s Leaders”.
