Labs

Hi-tech computer laboratories featuring advanced systems and high-speed internet access, ensuring that students are well-versed in technology and digital learning.

Library

A well-equipped library with an extensive collection of books on a wide range of subjects, including languages, literature, science, history, geography, economics, computers, philosophy, religion, business studies, accounts, mathematics, and encyclopedias.

Laboratories

Modern laboratories for Physics, Chemistry, and Mathematics, stocked with advanced equipment that allow students to engage in hands-on experiments and deepen their understanding of scientific concepts

Music Room

A dedicated space for students passionate about music, where they can explore various instruments and musical genres.

Indoor and Outdoor Games

We offer a wide range of outdoor games with courts and grounds for Basketball, Gymnasium, Volleyball, Badminton, and Tennis, as well as fields for Kho-Kho, Football, and Cricket, providing ample opportunities for students to engage in physical activities and sportsmanship.

Medical Room

A fully-equipped medical room that provides immediate first aid and medical attention when necessary, ensuring the health and safety of our students.

Transport Facilities

The school operates its own fleet of buses, ensuring safe and reliable transportation for students.
