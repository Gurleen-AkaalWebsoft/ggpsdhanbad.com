Guru Gobind Singh Public School, Dhanbad

Welcome to GGPS, Dhanbad

Guru Gobind Singh Public School is a place dedicated to world class education, focused on educating India, backing student success and responding to the needs of society. Its high level of research is evidenced by numerous awards and its position in National rankings. But GGPS, Dhanbad can also be proud of its leading position among many schools, in terms of integration of students. With ongoing intensive plateform in a wide range of fields, our school is particularly famous for its very high level in basic education.

Guru Gobind Singh Public School, Dhanbad, also takes great pride in its holistic approach to education. The school emphasizes not only academic excellence but also the development of key life skills, fostering creativity, leadership, and social responsibility in its students. With state-of-the-art facilities, dedicated faculty, and a strong focus on extracurricular activities, GGPS ensures that students are well-prepared to face the challenges of a rapidly changing world. The school's commitment to nurturing well-rounded individuals reflects in the remarkable achievements of its alumni, who excel in various fields both nationally and internationally.

GGPS Dhanbad is committed to equal opportunity for all individuals in education.

Our Objectives

The Primary Objectives of GGPS

Quality Employability Focus

Nurturing students from diverse backgrounds into highly employable individuals, through personalized attention and skill-building

Bridging Local and Global Knowledge

Developing a multi-disciplinary curriculum that integrates contemporary global advancements to ensure students stay ahead

Comprehensive Student Evaluation

A holistic evaluation system involving quizzes, seminars, projects, and workshops to foster continuous growth and learning


Leveraging Technology for Excellence

Using cutting-edge technology to help students track their progress, ensuring constant improvement and performance optimization

Merit and Need-Based Scholarships

Offering scholarships to recognize excellence and provide financial support to students from economically disadvantaged backgrounds

Empowering Social Responsibility

Promoting activities with social significance, encouraging students to embrace social responsibility and contribute to global issues


Our Core Values

  • Empowering Student Success

    We provide the tools and support for every student to reach their full potential.

  • Commitment to Lifelong Learning

    We inspire continuous growth, both inside and outside the classroom, for lifelong success.

  • Building a Foundation of Integrity and Respect

    We foster an environment where honesty, trust, and fairness are at the core of everything we do.

  • Striving for Excellence Every Day

    We pursue the highest standards in education, constantly improving to shape a brighter future for our students.
